Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
